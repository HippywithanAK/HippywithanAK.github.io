Aims for this blog

This blog should serve as a place to practice and implement the skills I learn while at Dev Academy.

I should like to use it as a record of my learning and will aim to keep a journal, where I shall make daily entries. Here I will lay out the aims for my day and reflect on my progress when I knock off.

I would also like to have it serve as a sort of repository for learning materials, cheatsheets, flowcharts, video tutorials and the like.
